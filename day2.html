Day 2: Face Your Baseline

Welcome to the 5-day warm-up from Run the Ladder! For 15 minutes a day, you'll size up your career and take the first steps towards improved fitness, starting now.

  • Day 1: Pick Your Target
  • Day 2: Face Your Baseline
  • Day 3: Chart Your Network
  • Day 4: Check Your Routine
  • Day 5: Take The First Step

No training program can start without understanding your baseline. Take 15 minutes and write answers to these questions about your current state:

  • What's your strongest asset right now (skill, win, or role)?
  • What's one risk or gap threatening your Day 1 target?
  • How long have you been coasting or pushing at your current level?
  • What's one move you've made lately that helps or hurts your Day 1 target?
  • How ready are you today (1-10) for your Day 1 target?

Be real, this isn't for show. We all have to sometimes face the reality of where we are in the journey to start to make progress forwards. Revisit your answers tomorrow, do they hold up?
