4-Week Plan to Routine

You're mid-career in tech. You're good at what you do. But when someone asks "what's next?" you don't have a clear answer. You know you should be more intentional about your career, but between the day job and life, it never happens.

This 4-week program gives you what you're missing: a concrete plan for where you're going and a routine to actually get there.

  1. Week 1 (45 min) - Career fitness assessment: we diagnose where you are across goal clarity, network strength, impact visibility, and industry awareness
  2. Week 2 (45 min) - Your career plan: a real, written plan for your next career move
  3. Week 3 (45 min) - Your routine: the specific habits and actions that will get you there
  4. Week 4 (45 min) - Integration: putting it all together so it actually sticks

What you get: 3 hours of 1-on-1 coaching, structured assignments between sessions, and async support when you need it.

What you leave with: A plan you believe in and a routine you can actually follow.
