The Program

The Search

Most engineers are good at their job but improvise in a job search. The Search changes that.

I've interviewed and hired engineers, and designed hiring processes. I've also recently executed my own job search and know the current market challenges first hand. We'll create a routine around where you are right now, focused on conversations, targeted applications, online presence, and interview prep for the AI age.

Borrowing proven approaches from fitness training, the best results come from consistency over time, not one intense or fragmented effort. Running a successful job search works the same way, and starts with a strong foundation.

What's Included

  • 01Build Your Foundation

    Career Fitness, why routine beats motivation, building your target company list, optimizing your resume & LinkedIn, and your parallel goal.

  • 02Gain Momentum

    Defining your weekly scorecard, sourcing opportunities with AI, networking that actually works, and creating content to build visibility.

  • 03Win Interviews

    Going from surviving to winning interviews, technical and behavioral interview strategy, mock interviews, and the best tools and services for interview prep.

  • 04Beyond the Sprint

    Maintaining the routine, learning from rejection, knowing when to change your approach, avoiding burnout, building your support system, and working effectively with recruiters.
