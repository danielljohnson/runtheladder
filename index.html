4-Week Career Fitness Gains

What if you were confident in the direction and resilience of your career? What if you knew you were ready for a sudden change? What if it only took 15 minutes a day to get there? This program is for mid-career tech professionals who are tired of feeling adrift in their career and want clarity, structure, and routine to confidently move forward.

  1. Week 1 - Career fitness analysis (Goal Clarity, Network Strength, Impact Visibility, Industry Awareness)
  2. Week 2 - A real, on-paper career plan for your current goal

  4. Week 4 - Putting it all together
