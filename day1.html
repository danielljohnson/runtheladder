Day 1: Pick Your Target

Welcome to the 5-day warm-up from Run the Ladder! For 15 minutes a day, you'll size up your career fitness and build an elevator pitch, starting now.

  • Day 1: Pick Your Target
  • Day 2: Face your baseline
  • Day 3: Map your crew
  • Day 4: Check your routine
  • Day 5: Take the first step

First up: define what you're optimizing for right now in your career. Career goals change over time and there's always tradeoffs to be made. Write down answers to the following questions within the context of the next year:

  • What's your top career priority?
  • How long have you been trying to achieve this?
  • What things are you ok with putting aside to achieve it?
  • Does it fit your life outside work?
  • What's your risk tolerance (1-10) to achieve this goal?

Come back later today or early tomorrow and review what you wrote, does it still sound right? Often we give a snap response and answers shift, our goal is to get to clarity.

First rep done! You've taken a small part of your day and focused on your career. Day 2 hits your inbox tomorrow!
