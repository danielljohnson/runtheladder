Day 1: Pick Your Target

Welcome to the 5-day warm-up from Run the Ladder! For 15 minutes a day, you'll size up your career and take the first steps towards improved fitness, starting now.

First up: define what you're optimizing for right now in your career. Career goals change over time and there are always tradeoffs to be made. Write down answers to the following questions within the context of the next year:

  • What's your top career priority?
  • How long have you been trying to achieve this?
  • What's one career move you'd pause to make this happen?
  • Does this target fit with your life outside work?
  • How confident are you (1-10) you can pull this off?

Come back later today or early tomorrow and review what you wrote. Does it still sound right? Often we give a snap response. Answers can shift, our goal is clarity.

First rep done! You've taken a small part of your day and focused on your career. Day 2 hits your inbox tomorrow!
