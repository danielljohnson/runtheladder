Day 3: Chart Your Network

Welcome to the 5-day warm-up from Run the Ladder! For 15 minutes a day, you'll size up your career and take the first steps towards improved fitness, starting now.

  • Day 1: Pick Your Target
  • Day 2: Face Your Baseline
  • Day 3: Chart Your Network
  • Day 4: Check Your Routine
  • Day 5: Take The First Step

No career is built solo. Your network's your crew. Take 15 minutes and write answers to these questions about who's in your orbit:

  • List 3 people outside your current job who know your work best, and how could they support your Day 1 target?
  • Find one connection on LinkedIn you've lost touch with who could boost your Day 1 target, send them a message to catch up.
  • Who's one person you trust to give you real feedback on your Day 1 target, and why them?
  • Name one person who's ahead of you on a path similar to your Day 1 target, what did they do to get there?
  • How strong is your network today (1-10) for your Day 1 target?

Networks are the foundation of solid careers. Revisit your list tomorrow, miss anyone?
